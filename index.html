SERVICES

Flat Roofs

Flat roofing is a popular choice for modern buildings, offering a sleek design and practical benefits. It's relatively cost-effective, and provides extra usable space. With proper maintenance, flat roofs are durable and can efficiently protect your property from the elements.

Commercial Roofs

Commercial roofing is essential for protecting large-scale buildings, ensuring durability and energy efficiency. Designed to withstand heavy use and varying weather conditions. With the right materials and professional installation, commercial roofing offers long-term protection and helps maintain the value of your property.

Pitch Roofs

Pitched roofs are known for their durability and efficient water drainage. Their sloped design not only enhances the aesthetic appeal of a building but also provides extra attic space and better insulation. With a variety of materials available, pitched roofs offer long-lasting protection and can withstand harsh weather conditions.

Repairs

Roof repairs are crucial for maintaining the integrity and longevity of your roof. Whether addressing minor leaks or fixing damages, timely repairs prevent small issues from becoming costly problems. Professional roof repair ensures your home or building stays protected from the elements, extending the life of your roof and preserving your property’s value.

Leadworks

Leadwork is a traditional roofing technique valued for its durability and flexibility. Often used in historic buildings and intricate roof designs, lead provides excellent weather resistance and a long lifespan. Its malleability allows for precise detailing around chimneys, dormers, and other roof features, making it both a practical and aesthetically pleasing choice for long-term protection.

Heritage

Heritage roofing preserves the character and integrity of historic buildings while providing modern protection. Specializing in traditional materials and techniques, heritage roofing ensures that repairs and restorations blend seamlessly with the original structure. This approach not only safeguards the building’s historical significance but also extends its lifespan, keeping its legacy alive for future generations.
